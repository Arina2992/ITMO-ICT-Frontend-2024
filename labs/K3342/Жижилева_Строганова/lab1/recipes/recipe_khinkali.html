Khinkali

Try it if you like Georgia

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Meat
  • Water
Steps:
  1. Make dough: Mix flour, water, and salt to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare filling: Mix ground beef or pork with onions, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper, and water or broth.
  3. Shape dumplings: Roll out dough into circles. Place filling in the center, then pinch and twist the edges to seal.
  4. Boil: Cook Khinkali in salted boiling water until they float, about 7-10 minutes.
  5. Serve: Drain and serve hot, traditionally eaten by hand.
