Tom Yum

Author: John Doe

Favorite dish from Thailand

Ingredients:
  • Shrimp
  • Lemon grass
  • mushroom
Steps:
  1. Boil broth: Heat shrimp broth with lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves.
  2. Add aromatics: Stir in Thai chili paste, fish sauce, and lime juice.
  3. Cook protein: Add shrimp or chicken and cook until done.
  4. Add veggies: Toss in mushrooms and tomatoes, simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Finish: Garnish with fresh cilantro and chili.
