Hungry in the morning?

Ingredients:
  • Bacon
  • Sausage
  • Egg
  • Beans
  • Toast
Steps:
  1. Cook sausages: Fry or grill sausages until browned and cooked through.
  2. Cook bacon: Fry bacon until crispy.
  3. Prepare eggs: Fry or scramble eggs to your liking.
  4. Cut sausage in slices.
  5. Heat beans: Warm baked beans in a small pan.
  6. Toast bread: Toast slices of bread and butter them.
  7. Serve: Plate everything with optional black pudding and hash browns.
