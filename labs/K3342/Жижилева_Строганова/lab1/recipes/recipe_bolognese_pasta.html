Bolognese Pasta

Spaghetti bolognese time

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
Steps:
  1. Cook pasta: Boil your choice of pasta (spaghetti, tagliatelle) in salted water until al dente.
  2. Sauté base: In a pan, sauté diced onions, garlic, and carrots in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add meat
  4. Add liquids: Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, red wine, and beef stock. Simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Season: Add salt, pepper, and Italian herbs to taste. Optionally, stir in a splash of milk or cream for richness.
  6. Serve: Toss the sauce with pasta and top with grated Parmesan.
