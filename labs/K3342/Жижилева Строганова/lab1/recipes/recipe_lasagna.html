Lasagna

Easy everyday lasagna:)

Ingredients:
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
Steps:
  1. Prepare meat sauce: Sauté onions and garlic, then brown ground beef. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  2. Make cheese mixture: Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente and drain.
  4. Layer lasagna: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
