Fish and Chips

You have to try it!

Ingredients:
  • Fish
  • Potatoes
  • Batter
Steps:
  1. Prepare potatoes: Cut potatoes into fries. Soak in water, then dry and fry at 325°F until soft. Set aside.
  2. Make batter: Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and cold beer (or sparkling water) into a thick batter.
  3. Prepare fish: Season fish fillets (cod or haddock) with salt and pepper, then dip in the batter.
  4. Fry fish: Deep-fry battered fish in hot oil (350°F) until golden and crispy, about 4-5 minutes.
  5. Double-fry chips: Fry the potatoes again at 375°F until crispy.
  6. Serve: Plate the fish and chips with tartar sauce and lemon wedges.
